FinTech for Good

Helping the Homeless

Presenting By - Kleos (13)

TD’s Purpose

Enrich the lives of customers, communities & colleagues

Problem

There is a gap in providing those in need with funding and access to daily necessities (Feminine Hygiene, Personal Care, Clothing)

Opportunity

  • Provide TD’s 5.2mm mobile customers the opportunities to donate through the app by leveraging existing channels
  • Chalmers (Ample Labs) is chatbot connecting homeless people to vital resources that we sample as a use case

Solution

Leverage the Chalmers channel to collect donations via TD mobile experience and partner with relevant retailers. Redemption offers will be available to homeless users.

UX Prototype
Chalmers.app

As a person in need, I can redeem an offer presented to me via Chalmers by visiting a supporting retailer

As a TD Customer, I am notified via the TD mobile app when I help a person so that I know my donations are making a difference

Technology

Integrate with TD and Chalmers to build new API endpoints and services

Next step

  • Pilot program with small group of TD users that have previously expressed interest in donating
  • Partner with local retailers in an area of need and define initial product catalogue
  • Develop API for integration with Chalmers app to provide data feed of products available for purchase with donated funds
