Problem
Beyond food and shelter, there is a gap in providing those in need with funding and access to necessities:

  • Feminine hygiene products
  • Personal care products
  • Food products
  • Clothing products

Opportunity
  • Leverage existing channels to collect donations via TD mobile experience
  • Partner with retailers such as pharmacy, grocery and clothing chains
  • Build middleware between TD / retailers / apps like Chalmers to surface redemption offers at participating locations
  • Introduce capability for people to share charitable moments on social to encourage others
UX Prototype
Chalmers.app

As a person in need, I can redeem an offer presented to me via Chalmers by visiting a supporting retailer

As a TD donator, I am notified via the TD mobile app when I help a person so that I know my donations are making a difference

Technology

Integrate with TD and Chalmers to build new API endpoints and services

Next step

  • Pilot program with small group of TD users that have previously expressed interest in donating
  • Partner with local retailers in an area of need and define initial product catalogue
  • Develop API for integration with Chalmers app to provide data feed of products available for purchase with donated funds
